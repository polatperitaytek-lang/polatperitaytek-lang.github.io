HOW TO GET TIERTESTED
Want an official tier? Follow the steps below and prove your skill in competitive Minecraft PvP.
01
APPLY
Reach out to the tier list staff through the official channels to apply for a tier test.
02
PICK GAMEMODE
Choose a gamemode — Vanilla, UHC, Pot, Sword, Axe, Mace, NethOP, or SMP.
03
GET TESTED
A tester will be assigned to evaluate your gameplay and skill level in your chosen mode.
04
GET RANKED
Based on performance, you'll receive a tier from HT1 (elite) down to LT5. Retest to improve!
